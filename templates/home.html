Welcome Message

Welcome to the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at the University of Chittagong. As the Chairman of this prestigious department, it is my pleasure to introduce you to our community of scholars, innovators, and future leaders in the field of computer science and engineering.

At the Department of CSE, we strive to continue a rigorous academic environment that promotes both learning and research. Our curriculum is designed to equip students with foundational knowledge in computer science, while also fostering innovation in areas such as artificial intelligence, data science, cybersecurity, software engineering, and more. We believe in nurturing critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and technical expertise to prepare our students for the ever-evolving global tech landscape.

Our faculty members are dedicated to academic excellence and cutting-edge research, collaborating with both national and international institutions to contribute to advancements in computer science. We aim to inspire our students to push boundaries and excel not only academically but also as professionals who can make meaningful contributions to society.

We are proud of the strong sense of community within our department, where faculty, staff, and students work together to achieve academic and professional success. Whether you are a prospective student, a researcher, or an industry partner, we invite you to explore the various opportunities that our department offers and become a part of our journey towards shaping the future of technology.

Thank you for visiting our website.
